# Paraglider Landing Pattern
A small browser app for calculating and displaying the landing pattern for paragliders.

I completed my paragliding course in 2024 and initially struggled to estimate the required altitude for entering the landing pattern. I’ve since developed a feel for it, but during my training it would have been helpful to have a small programme that allowed me to adjust the parameters affecting the landing and calculate the impact on the required altitude and the downward angle of aim to the target.

This browser app allows students to adjust parameters such as the leg lengths of the landing pattern, the wind in the landing zone and the glider’s flight characteristics, and to see the effect on the landing pattern. The whole thing is displayed in a top-down view and in isometric projection, and can be rotated and zoomed.
